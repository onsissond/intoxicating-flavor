The Intoxicating Flavor
Interactive Visual Novel

Overview

In this unique narrative, we delve into the tale of a young man stranded on a remote island, cut off from the world and resigned to a monotonous existence awaiting rescue. However, the arrival of unexpected visitors alters the course of his solitary days. As laughter fills the air and intriguing situations unfold, the tranquility of the night is disturbed by peculiar dreams, unveiling the complexity of the island's secrets. What began as a lighthearted exploration of newfound relationships soon evolves into a profound exploration of individual fears and hidden truths. Each character's decisions carry unforeseen ramifications, challenging preconceptions and leading to unforeseeable outcomes.

Key Features

  • Interactive Storytelling
  • Dystopian Realism
  • Morality Dilemmas
  • Multidimensional Characters
